Davend Punch & Die Co. Ltd is a Mississauga based company established in 1993, by two partners. Now fully run by Paresh Patel, one of the original partners. Davend Punch offers 30+ years of experience in manufacturing punches and die bushings/buttons to fit all our customers needs.

We specialize in a variety of punches, die buttons/bushings, centerless grinding, ID grinding, and surface grinding for a variety of industries.
